# Multi-Tenant Business Model & Revenue Strategy

## 🎯 Business Overview

Transform your single-school system into a **profitable SaaS platform** hosting multiple schools with recurring revenue streams.

### **Pricing Tiers**

| Plan | Price/Month | Students | Features | Target Market |
|------|-------------|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Basic** | $50 | Up to 500 | • Student Management<br>• Teacher Management<br>• Basic Reports<br>• Email Support | Small Schools |
| **Premium** | $100 | Up to 2,000 | • All Basic Features<br>• Attendance Tracking<br>• Fee Management<br>• WhatsApp Integration<br>• Priority Support | Medium Schools |

- **Decision Maker**: Board + IT Director

## 🛠️ Operational Model

#### **Direct Sales**
- **Cost**: $0 (you do the selling)
- **Conversion**: 5-10% (high-touch)
- **Time**: 2-4 weeks per school

#### **Referral Program**
- **Cost**: 10% of first year revenue
